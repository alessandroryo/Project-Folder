Choosing HBO-ICT

I chose HBO-ICT at HZ University of Applied Science, because since I was a kid, I really liked computers and games. Then I really wanted to study in the Netherlands, far from Indonesia. Studying at HZ would fit my hopes and dreams very well. For more experience see my blog.

My other opinions...

Passion

Choosing HBO-ICT is my life choice and my passion is in this major. I also had the opportunity to be a head of IT division in the student council, where I created a website for the student council, became a social media manager, and had several other tasks related to the ICT world.

Successful Figure

I see many examples of successful figures because of their expertise in the ICT world. For examples, Bill Gates, Steve Jobs, Mark Zuckerberg, Larry Page, and many more. From there I learned a lot about the IT world and its struggles.

Parent Experience

My father have worked in IT for more than 20 years and I am often told about my father day-to-day experiences working in IT. I learned a lot of hardships at work as well as the joys that come with it.
